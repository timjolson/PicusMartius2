Notes: 
1 or 0 are bool flags
# are integers
#.# are floats
everything is case-sensitive

To ROV
	Joystick data
		###x###y##z
	Controller data
		###a###r##w
	Controller D-Pad (how is it formatted?)
		d######
	Pneumatics a/A/G/g are cylinders
		a1
		a0
		A1
		A0
		G1
		G0
		g1
		g0
	Closed loop orientation control on or off
		O1
		O0
	Closed loop depth control on or off
		D1
		D0
	Closed loop yaw control on or off
		Y1
		Y0
	Reset alarms, over or undercurrent
		M
	Enable all thrusters for diving, etc.
		m
	ROV at surface
		s //turn off vertical thrusters
		S //turn off all thrusters
	Camera switching
		C# //feed 1 camera #
		c# //feed 2 camera #
	Pump on or off
		P1
		P0
	PID adjustments (divide by 1000 upon receive)
		Dp####
		Di####
		Dd####
		Tp####
		Ti####
		Td####
		Yp####
		Yi####
		Yd####
	Save PID values
		%
	Calibrate IMU
		&
	
	
	Text entry in GUI should feed anything directly down

	Orientation data (angles in degrees)

		Z###
	Regulator temps (degrees C)
		T##.#,##.#
	Pump IS on or off
		P1
		P0
	Temperature from sensor (degrees C? then millis timestamp)
		t##.#,######
	Pressure from pressure sensor (mbar?)
		d##.#
	Camera settings
		C#,#
		Feed 1 camera selection, feed 2 selection
	Current limits
		m# //undercurrent with motor number

		Di#.###

		Tp#.###
		Ti#.###
		Td#.###
		Yp#.###
		Yi#.###
